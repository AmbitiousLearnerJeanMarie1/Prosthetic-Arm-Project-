# Prosthetic-Arm-Project-
Designed and 3D printed a prosthetic arm as part of a team of 10 students through the WNY STEM Hub program. The project aimed to create a functional assistive  arm for a 9-year-old athlete.

 My Contributions
- Assisted in design and prototyping of the prosthetic arm
- Contributed to 3D printing and assembly of components
- Collaborated with team members to improve functionality and fit

Tools & Technologies
- 3D Printing
- CAD Design
- Prototyping
- Team Collaboration

 Impact
The project was recognized by the Buffalo News for its innovation and impact, helping provide a practical solution for a young athlete.
